Services

Every company is different, which is why we take the time to meet with you and discuss your needs. We can then work with you to form a plan to meet your business goals and budget. Whether you are looking for a short term project partner, or a long term support team, we can help. We service the Tri-State area as well as customers across the United States and Canada. Using our technology and the Internet as a conduit we can accommodate customers needs while keeping your costs manageable.

I.T. Consulting

We bring over twelve years of Systems experience to your business, and partner with other proven experts in the field to provide the perfect solution for your business. We have experience in the medical, legal, collections, real estate and other business sub-specialties including IT based business. Whether you are looking for project management, advanced technology solutions or outsourced system implementation, our consultants can help

HelpDesk Services

We offer a complete HelpDesk solution for all clients using our Network Support, Managed Services and Hosted Service offerings. Our HelpDesk solution is state of the art. You can submit tickets via telephone or email, view and update your requests online and view previous tickets for recurring issues. Managers can be in touch with all your HelpDesk issues... being updated via email of new office requests or simply being able to log into the HelpDesk Control Panel and review requests submitted by staff. Our HelpDesk is not only a tool for us to efficiently handle your issues, but allows you to identify ongoing problems in your organization. Our HelpDesk combined with Remote Support and Network Monitoring system provides a powerful tool to support your business and address small issues before they become larger business interruptions.
