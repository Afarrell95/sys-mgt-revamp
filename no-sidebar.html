Services

Every company is different, which is why we take the time to meet with you and discuss your needs. We can then work with you to form a plan to meet your business goals and budget. Whether you are looking for a short term project partner, or a long term support team, we can help. We service the Tri-State area as well as customers across the United States and Canada. Using our technology and the Internet as a conduit we can accommodate customers needs while keeping your costs manageable.

We provide the following services:

I.T. Consulting

We bring over twelve years of Systems experience to your business, and partner with other proven experts in the field to provide the perfect solution for your business. We have experience in the medical, legal, collections, real estate and other business sub-specialties including IT based business.

We are experienced in information technology solutions including HIPAA, Security, Network design, Implementation and Management, Data Redundancy and Backup Solutions. Whether you are looking for project management, advanced technology solutions or outsourced system implementation, our consultants can help

Network Support

Does your business already have HelpDesk or other technical staff but you need assistance with Advanced Network Support? Do you need assistance with Server Installation, Firewall Setup, Remote VPN connectivity or do you simply need Network compliance management? We offer Network Support services for this exact reason. Your don't need us for the small issues, but you don't want those small issues to become big ones. Contact us now and we can outline an affordable partnership that allows you to keep costs in line without letting your network get out of line.

HelpDesk Services

We offer a complete HelpDesk solution for all clients using our Network Support, Managed Services and Hosted Service offerings. Our HelpDesk solution is state of the art. You can submit tickets via telephone or email, view and update your requests online and view previous tickets for recurring issues. Managers can be in touch with all your HelpDesk issues... being updated via email of new office requests or simply being able to log into the HelpDesk Control Panel and review requests submitted by staff.

Our HelpDesk is not only a tool for us to efficiently handle your issues, but allows you to identify ongoing problems in your organization. Our HelpDesk combined with Remote Support and Network Monitoring system provides a powerful tool to support your business and address small issues before they become larger business interruptions.

Managed Services

Are you looking for a Managed Service Partner? Some companies have decided to outsource their I.T. needs allowing better efficiency, more experienced network support and up to date technologies. We work with your company to provide total systems management including Network Support and Management, HelpDesk Services, and complete system monitoring. Your dedicated I.T. Resource staff can work on-site or remotely to fit your business and budget needs.

Hosted Services

  • DataLock Online Backup:

    Our premier product, DataLock Backup allows your business to store its essential data off-site for disaster recovery online using our secure Datacenter. We provide expert installation support and configuration. Whether looking for an alternative to tapes, meeting compliance regulations or simply proactively protecting your business, DataLock is a great solution.

  • Server and Network Monitoring:

    Server and Network monitoring allows us to know immediately if there is a problem with the critical components of your network. No more need to decide if you need to call for support. We call you and can quickly identify and fix the problem.

  • Remote Support:

    We have the ability to support you remotely wherever you are. We can quickly reboot a server, remove a virus or simply help you with an application installation.With remote support we can help you instantly.

  • SmarterMail Email:

    Smarter Email is for users looking for basic email for their business but who don't need all the advanced features of Exchange or Outlook. Smartermail is a web based email system that also allows POP and IMAP access.

  • Website Hosting:

    We offer premium web hosting. Our web servers have many advanced features including DotNetPanel Control access, FTP, MySQL, PHP 5.1, PERL, ASP, .Net 3.5, and many auto-installers to quickly get your site up and running.

  • Website Statistics:

    SmarterStats is a premium web statistics package that allows your business to interpret visitor traffic to your site. By analyzing your SmarterStats information you can make key decisions related to website improvements, SEO optimization and keyword advertising.
